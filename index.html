About

Some things exist in the space between — a feeling without a name, an idea that hasn't become a song yet. That's where I work.

I'm Reece. Songwriter, producer and topliner based in Aotearoa, New Zealand. I write concept-first songs for artists moving between intimacy, scale, and emotional ambiguity.

liminl. is where those songs gather: cinematic pop, left-of-centre ideas, and demos built to start conversations with artists, managers, and collaborators.

How I Work

01

Cinematic Emotional Pop

Big feeling, slow-burn tension, and emotional payoff. Songs written for artists who want atmosphere, vulnerability, and a chorus that feels like it opens a door.

02

Conceptual / Left-of-Centre Pop

Sharper concepts, stranger angles, and lyrics with a point of view. Songs built around identity, media, perception, and the stories people perform to survive.

03

Hypnotic Dark Pop

Slow builds, unsettling beauty, and hooks that arrive like a bruise. Songs for artists who want their darkness to feel deliberate — not decorative.

04

Sync & Screen

Emotionally precise songs built to carry a moment — trailers, title sequences, TV drama, and brand campaigns. Written with flexibility for picture.
